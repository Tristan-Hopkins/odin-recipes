Grandmas Famous Potato Casserole Recipe

This potato casserole is a creamy and comforting side dish that's perfect for any occasion. Made with simple ingredients like potatoes, cheese, and sour cream, it's easy to prepare and always a crowd-pleaser. Follow these step-by-step instructions to create a delicious potato casserole that your family and friends will love!

  • 4 cups peeled and thinly sliced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Now that you have all the ingredients ready, let's start making the potato casserole!

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Layer the sliced potatoes and chopped onion in a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, milk, melted butter, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  4. Pour the cheese mixture over the potatoes and onion, making sure to coat them evenly.
  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  6. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the potatoes are tender.
  7. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley over the top (optional) and let the casserole cool for a few minutes before serving.
