Grandama's Famous Lasanga

Today, I'm going to show you how to make a classic and delicious lasagna recipe that's been passed down through generations in my family. This lasagna is made with hearty ground beef, a rich tomato sauce, and a creamy cheese mixture, all layered between tender lasagna noodles. It's sure to become a favorite in your family too! Get ready to learn how to make grandma's famous lasagna.

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) of your favorite pasta sauce
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    •

    now that you have all of the ingredients ready, it's time to get started on making this delicious lasagna! Follow these step-by-step instructions to create a homemade masterpiece that will delight your taste buds and impress your family and friends. Let's get cooking!

    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the beef is browned and the onion is tender.
    3. Add the pasta sauce, tomato sauce, basil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    4. Meanwhile, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
    5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1-1/2 cups of the mozzarella cheese, and 1/4 cup of the Parmesan cheese.
    6. In a 13x9 inch baking dish, spread 1 cup of the meat sauce. Layer 4 of the cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread 1/2 of the cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat the layers of sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture. Finally, top with the remaining sauce.
    7. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese over the top of the lasagna.
    8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
    9. Let the lasagna cool for 5-10 minutes before cutting and serving.
